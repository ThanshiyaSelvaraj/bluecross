About Blue Cross

Blue Cross is a non-profit organization dedicated to animal welfare.

  • Animal Rescue: Blue Cross rescues stray, abandoned, and injured animals, providing them with the necessary care and rehabilitation.
  • Adoption Services: They facilitate the adoption process by connecting animals with loving families.
  • Veterinary Care: Providing medical treatment, vaccinations, and spay/neuter programs to reduce suffering and control animal populations.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Blue Cross promotes awareness on animal rights, the importance of spaying/neutering, and responsible pet ownership
